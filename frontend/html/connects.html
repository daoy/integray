About Accenture Connects

Accenture Connects is a mobile first application designed specifically to enhance our employee engagement throughout our company with Gamification features. These Gamification features will be in the form of challenges, which every user has the ability to create, add colleagues, and earn points and badges. Other features of this application revolve entirely around the challenges. Any type of challenge can be created. Whether it's fitness, social, work related, philanthropic, or miscellaneous. Here at Accenture we shape the culture and define the character of our company. Fulfilling our vision to improve the way the world works and lives means playing a vital role in bringing together our people, clients, suppliers and partners to tackle the complex challenges and opportunities that lay ahead. While using this application, create challenges, compete, earn badges, upload images and engage with your Accenture family whether your working from home or in one of our global locations. Enjoy!
